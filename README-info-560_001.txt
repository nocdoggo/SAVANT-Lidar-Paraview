NCAR/EOL 5 minute ISFS surface flux data, geographic coordinate and tilt
corrected winds
https://data.eol.ucar.edu/dataset/560.001

Summary
This dataset contains quality controlled 5 minute averages of surface flux
data collected by the NCAR/EOL Integrated Surface Flux System (ISFS) during
the Stable Air Variability and Transport (SAVANT) field campaign. These data
have been quality controlled and are available in NetCDF format. The winds
reported by the sonic anemometers have been rotated into geographic
coordinates and tilt corrected relative to a surface plane using a Leica
Multistation (laser theodolite).
